Pictured above are some of the posters of the longest running shows.

The longer a show runs, the more succesful the show becomes. This is because more people are able to see the show and listen to the music, etc. However, even if a show does not have a long run, it can still be succesful. For example, "The Prom" and "The Cher Show" both ran for under a year on Broadway, but they are still very popular and well known.

There are also some upcoming shows that were succesful enough to transition to Broadway. An example of this is "Six," a musical based on King Henry's six wives. This musical was very anticipated, so a lot of tickets have already sold before the musical even came to Broadway.
